Don Jose Julian Marti was born in Havana Cuba on January 28, 1853. On October 4, 1869 at the home of Fermín Valdés Domínguez, Martí (age 17) and other young men are taken into custody by a group of Spanish Volunteers after a letter is found opposing Spanish rule. They were accused of treason and taken to a Havana jail on October 21, 1869. He left his mark everywhere he went from Spain, Mexico, Guatemala, Honduras, and the US. His words were an inspiration to people all over the world.
